Welcome to Wikiguessr

Warning: This game will not work properly (at all) with an ad-blocker enabled. I promise I won't show you any ads!


This game is a web-based recreation of WikiArena by Fabian Fischer. Two random Wikipedia articles will apear on the screen. You will have two options to select for each article. You can guess that one article has recieved more views in the last month, or you can guess that one article is longer. You have 3 lives, and your goal is to survive 10 rounds. Good luck!
